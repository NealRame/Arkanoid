### Changes
- Ball speed increases when it hits the roof.
- Ball speed slows down when it hits the Vaus.
- Ball bounce on the Vaus as in the original game.
- Power Up
  - Ball disruption, 
  - Catch ball,
  - Expand,
  - Extra life,
  - Laser,
  - Slow ball speed.
- You earn an extra life every 20000 points.
